Celeste

A journey to the top of the mountain

Celeste is a 2d plataform game originally lunched on January 2018. In the game you're Madeline, a girl who wants to get to the top of Celeste Mountain, getting through challenges presented both from the mountain and her inner self.

Celeste is a beautiful game with a beautiful story. Recommended for those who appreciate a well told human tale and also like a gaming challenge. The game mechanics are responsive and give you the feel of true control of Madeline's movements. As you proced through the game, you'll get closer to the main character as her story unvails.

Recommend you to play this game with a tissue by your side, tears can come at any moment.
